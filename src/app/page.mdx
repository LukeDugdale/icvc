## Say hello to ICVC {{ date: '2023-10-10T00:00Z' }}

In this release, we preview the Internet Computer Venture Capital whitepaper as well as the ICVC dApp and its core functionality.

ICVC will be built on the Internet Computer, with the aim of creating a truly decentralised on-chain investment DAO. This would not be possible on other chains.

### <SparkleIcon /> Quick read

- ICVC is a DAO-governed venture capital dApp on the Internet Computer.
- We leverage state-of-the-art blockchain technology to enable the entire VC to run 100% on-chain.
- ICVC will invest in Internet Computer ecosystem & ecosystem-adjacent projects & companies.
- The anticipated launch of ICVC on the Internet Computer Mainnet is Q1 2024.
